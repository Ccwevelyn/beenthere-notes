### Data Strategy
**<font style="color:#DF2A3F;">Strategy</font>**<font style="color:#DF2A3F;"> can be defined as a </font>**<font style="color:#DF2A3F;">plan of action or policy (regulation)</font>**<font style="color:#DF2A3F;"> designed to achieve a major or overall </font>**<font style="color:#DF2A3F;">aim(objective/goal)</font>**<font style="color:#DF2A3F;">.</font>

#### Data initiative
<font style="color:rgb(238, 240, 255);background-color:rgb(31, 31, 31);">A data strategy is the </font>**<font style="color:rgb(238, 240, 255);background-color:rgb(31, 31, 31);">high-level plan</font>**<font style="color:rgb(238, 240, 255);background-color:rgb(31, 31, 31);"> and framework for how an organization uses data to achieve its business goals, while a data initiative is a specific project or action taken to execute part of that strategy.</font>

数据战略是组织数据资产的**总体长期计划**，定义实现业务目标的技术、流程、人员和规则。<font style="color:rgb(238, 240, 255);"> </font>相比之下，数据计划是一个具体的、**有针对性的项目**或一系列行动，旨在实施该战略的部分内容或利用数据解决特定的业务问题<font style="color:rgb(238, 240, 255);"> </font>

##### What ?
**Data initiatives** 是**围绕数据目标**展开的整体项目或行动，而 **data mining** 是其中一种技术手段，用于从数据中发现隐藏的模式和信息

##### Benefits of **<u><font style="color:rgb(0,0,0);">Aligning data initiatives with business goals and objectives</font></u>****<font style="color:rgb(0,0,0);">:</font>**
Data Initiative -  具体的数据项目或活动  

确保你做的**每一个数据项目都服务于企业的目标 ** 

```plain
Data Strategy（数据战略）
   └── Align Initiatives with Business Goals（确保项目与目标对齐）
         └── Data Initiatives（具体项目执行）
```

1. 确保数据项目对**企业及其利益相关者**具有相关性、价值和影响力- 真正服务于企业目标  
2. 避免因与目标不切合带来的浪费
3. 为**数据项目和数据团队**建立清晰、一致的方向和目标。  
4. 支持基于证据的决策和创新  -  企业在做决策时依赖数据分析结果  

Data- Driven Decision Making -> Goal setting -- Based on evidance

5. **衡量并监控**对于目标做出的贡献

##### Strategic Scenarios **战略情境模型**
<font style="color:rgb(0, 0, 0);">情景规划是一种战略规划形式，它可以帮助企业</font>**<font style="color:rgb(0, 0, 0);">预见</font>**[**<font style="color:rgb(0, 93, 170);">未来可能发生的情景</font>**](https://www.smestrategy.net/blog/what-is-scenario-planning-and-how-to-use-it)<font style="color:rgb(0, 0, 0);">，并制定</font>**<font style="color:rgb(0, 0, 0);">灵活的长期战略</font>**<font style="color:rgb(0, 0, 0);">，为未来做好准备。情景规划最初是作为军事情报工具而开发的，但它的应用已广泛应用于许多其他领域，尤其是管理领域。</font>

<font style="color:rgb(0, 0, 0);">情景规划的本质在于识别不确定性并做好准备。</font>

**<font style="color:rgb(64, 64, 64);">data strategy is the high-level vision</font>**<font style="color:rgb(64, 64, 64);"> that </font><u><font style="color:rgb(64, 64, 64);">aligns data initiatives with business goals</font></u><font style="color:rgb(64, 64, 64);">. It's the </font>**<font style="color:rgb(64, 64, 64);">roadmap</font>**<font style="color:rgb(64, 64, 64);"> that defines how data will be used to support growth, innovation, and competitive advantage.</font>

+ <font style="color:rgb(22, 22, 22);">元数据的简单定义是“关于数据的数据”。也就是说，它提供</font>**<font style="color:rgb(22, 22, 22);">与数据本身内容无关</font>**<font style="color:rgb(22, 22, 22);">的数据细节。例如，一张包含近期客户订单的表格构成一个数据集，而关于该数据集的信息（例如，数据集的所有者或文件类型）则是元数据。</font>
+ <font style="color:rgb(22, 22, 22);">具体来说，想象一下一本书。这里的元数据包括作者、书名、出版日期和目录。这些信息并不提供实际数据——也就是书的文本——但它们确实提供了对书籍进行分类和了解其来源的重要细节。</font>

##### **<font style="color:rgb(0,0,0);">（RACI Matrix）Stakeholders【In the Data Governance】</font>**
分工工具 - **责任划分框架 ** 

+ RACI 是一种责任分配工具，能清晰界定谁负责、谁决策、谁提供意见、谁需要被告知  

| **R** | Responsible | 负责**执行任务的人**， 通常是项目组成员或技术人员   |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **A** | Accountable | 对任务**结果负最终责任**的人，有权批准或否决。如，项目经理   |
| **C** | Consulted | **提供建议或反馈的**人，参与讨论但 不负责具体操作或交付成果，不承担执行责任。如法律顾问   |
| **I** | Informed | 被告知进展的人，**不参与决策或执行**。如 高层管理、外部合作方   |

###### Benefits
| It provides clarity and transparency on** who is responsible for what** in data management processes, reducing confusion. | 它能清晰透明地说明在数据管理流程中谁负责什么，从而减少混乱。 |
| --- | --- |
| It enhances **collaboration** and **communication** among stakeholders, ensuring that everyone is aware of their roles and expectations and that they are aligned on the goals and objectives of data governance. | 它促进利益相关者之间的协作与沟通，确保每个人都清楚自己的角色与职责，并在数据治理目标上达成一致。 |
| It **streamlines** **decision-making and accountability**, ensuring that decisions are made by the appropriate stakeholders, and that they are supported by relevant information and feedback. | 它简化决策流程与责任归属，确保**由合适的人员做出决策，并获得相关信息与反馈支持**。 |
| It** improves efficiency and effectiveness**, ensuring that tasks are assigned to the most suitable stakeholders and that duplication of efforts and conflicts are avoided. | 它**提高效率与效果**，确保任务分配给最合适的人员，避免重复劳动与冲突。 |
| It supports and enables **accountability,** ensuring that data governance activities are performed by the appropriate stakeholders. | 它支持并**强化责任归属**，确保数据治理活动由合适的人员执行。 |
| It **ensures that data quality issues are identified**, standards and regulations are followed, and that data quality rules and policies are enforced and resolved. | 它确保数据**质量问题被识别**，遵循相关标准与法规，并落实数据质量规则与政策。-  通过清晰的角色分配，数据治理中的质量问题才能被有效识别和解决   |




###### Challenge
| It requires a **clear understanding** of the **data governance framework**, including the data governance activities, stakeholders, and their relationships. | 需要对数据治理框架有清晰理解，包括治理活动、相关人员及其关系。 |
| --- | --- |
| It requires a** high level of commitment and involvement from all stakeholder**s, especially those who are accountable or responsible for data governance activities. | 需要所有相关人员高度投入，尤其是那些对数据治理活动负有责任或最终责任的人。 |
| It requires** regular review and update** to reflect changes in data governance requirements, processes, or stakeholders. | 需要定期审查和更新，以反映数据治理要求、流程或人员的变化。 |
| It may **not capture all complexities** of data governance situations, such as multiple levels of responsibility or accountability, or overlapping or conflicting roles. | 可能无法涵盖数据治理中的所有复杂情况，例如多层级责任、角色重叠或冲突。 |

##### Defining the Goals and creating a Roadmap
+ Your Roadmap should answer two Q：
    - **What** are you trying to accomplish？
    - **How **are you going to **get there？**
+ Ensure your objectives are S.M.A.R.T.:
    - Specific, measureable, attainable, relevant, and time-bound-有时限的
+ It should be very clear whether a milestone or deliverable was achieved; this should be able to be answered with a 'yes' or a 'no'
