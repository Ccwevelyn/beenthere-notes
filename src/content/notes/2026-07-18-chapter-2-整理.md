1. Two ways to view world
    1. **Selfish** point of view: consider only your own self and your core values 
    2. **Ethical** point of view: respect other people and their core values
2. Morality（道德）
    1. A society's rules of conduct 社会行为准则
    2. What people ought / ought not to do in various situations
3. Ethics（伦理）
    1. The philosophical study of morality 
    2. Rational examination into people's moral beliefs and behavior
    3. Ethics focuses on people's voluntary, moral choices make.
    4. **Ethical analysis**
        1. Ethics: rational, systematic 有条理的、系统的 analysis of conduct that can cause benefit or harm to other people
        2. _If it is an ethical question,we do ethical analysis._
        3. <u>Conclusions must be support.</u>(people are required to explain why) based on **<u><font style="color:#DF2A3F;">facts/values,shared values,logic</font></u>**

4. Ethical relativism/objectivism

**Ethical relativism**

+ Morality is a human **invention**/inside the human mind.
+  Moral values are subjective and dependent on individual or cultural perspectives.
+ Theory that there are no universal moral norms of right and wrong.
1. _Subjective relativism_

Each person decides right and wrong for himself or herself

2. _Cultural relativism_

What is "right" and "wrong" depends upon a society's actual moral guidelines



**Ethical objectivism**

Divine Command theory/ Ethical Egosim/Kantianism/Act Utilitarianism/Rule Utilitarianism/Social Contract Theory/Virtue Ethics

+ Morality has an existence outside the human mind, Our role as 

humans is to discover "the good."

+ Asserting that there are** objective moral principles** that apply universally to all individuals, regardless of personal beliefs or cultural norms.  -<font style="color:rgb(42, 43, 46);">认为存在客观的道德原则，无论个人信仰或文化规范如何，都普遍适用于所有人</font>

**Perfect/Imperfect Duties**

+ Perfect duty: duty obliged to fulfill without exception-必须履行 没有例外
+ Imperfect duty: duty obliged to fulfill in general but not in every instance
+ <font style="color:rgb(192,0,0);">If there is a conflict between a perfect duty and an imperfect duty, the perfect duty must prevail 占上风.</font>

**<font style="color:#5C0036;">Ans：</font>**

① (某行为) is a perfect duty ,which is absolute and must be fulfilled in all situations in Kantiansim.

②结合事例分析解释(示例中的哪些举动是这个行为?)

③【如有 optional】(某行为) is a imperfect duty,which obliged to fulfill in general but not in every instance.

④【optional】结合实例分析解释

⑤【optional】If there is a conflict between a perfect duty and an imperfect duty, the perfect duty must prevail

⑥Therefore, the action is morally wrong/right.
