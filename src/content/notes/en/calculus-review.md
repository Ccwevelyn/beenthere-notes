## Build the map first

Before doing problem sets, connect limits, derivatives, and integrals on one page. Write down what each formula is actually for.

## Practise by pattern

1. Start with one basic problem from each pattern.
2. Record where you got stuck, not just the final answer.
3. Return to mistakes two days later and solve them again.

## Before the exam

Collect the signs, domains, and intermediate steps you most often miss on a single checklist.
